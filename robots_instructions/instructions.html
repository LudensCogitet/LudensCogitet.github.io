Tap, drag, and release to move.

Short drag moves one space.

Longer drag jumps a space.

Collect all the microchips by moving onto or jumping over them. This will reveal the exit.

Move onto the exit to advance to the next level.

Moving into the same space as a robot or junk pile will cause it to disintegrate and you will lose a shield.

Moving into a robot or junk pile when you have 0 shields is game over.

Robots that move into the same space smash into junk! New junk piles produce energy crystals.

Collect 5 crystals to get an extra shield.

Jump a robot or junk pile to phase through it. The robot will be stunned.
Jump another robot or junk pile before the phase is complete to phase again!

This can be done indefinitely!
