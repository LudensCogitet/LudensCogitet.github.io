Hello. My name is John Hiner. I’m a husband, a father of four, a computer programmer, and an amature computer game developer. Which is to say I don’t have a whole lot of experience at it and I really do it for love as opposed to fame, or money, or whatever else.

The purpose of this weblog is to communicate what I’ve been working on and possibly what I’ve been thinking about in the realm of computer games. Why? Like I said, I love computer games. I want to be in on the conversation about them. I want to make games worth playing, and I want people to play them.

There are some very good ideas about games floating around, and some very bad ones too. Hopefully I can add more good ones than bad ones and maybe someone else will benefit along the way.
