Hi. I’m John Hiner. I’m a husband, a father, and a software engineer. I was born in 1987. I studied the classics and philosophy in school. I’ve played computer games since I was about 3 years old and I’ve thought I wanted to make computer games for nearly that long. It was only after the rather recent discovery that I love computer programming itself, regardless of the application, that my passion for game development began to bear fruit as a hobby.

I think computer games are a worthy art form, because if they aren’t we should probably stop playing and making them all the time.

This blog’s purpose is to give me a place to collect and express my thoughts without writing an absurdly long and awkward Twitter thread. Typically, these thoughts will be on the topics of programming and game design, but I’m not going to tie my hands or make any promises. :)

So, the things I write here are more or less for my benefit. But, if they can help you I would be delighted and if you want to have a conversation (even a heated one) I would be delighted again.

Cheers.
